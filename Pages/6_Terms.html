Savings & Shopper Card

  • Enjoy Free transactions at the till, no charge attached for each swipe of your card
  • Using this Savings Card is safer than carrying cash around with you.
  • If you top-up your Savings Card, it will remain valid for a period of 3 years from the date of top-up or upon full redemption, whichever occurs first.
  • No cash will be given on purchases made, your change will remain on the Savings Card.
  • Keep your Savings Car safe, there are replacement costs involved if you lose it.
  • Savings Card balances can be checked in store at the till or at the SuperCard kiosk.
  • You can choose an amount between R50 and R25 000 (max) to be loaded onto the Savings Card per month. Deposit often, not more than R5 000 a day.
  • You cannot draw cash from the Savings Card. It cannot be used at an ATM and no “cash back” option is available.
  • After every purchase, make sure that you get your card and receipt back.
  • If you do not use the funds on the Stokvel Card for a period of 3 years, the value remaining on the card wil not be refunded to you.
  • You will not be able to use the card to purchase goods on the internet or for services over the telephone.
  • Once a deposit is made at the till, it cannot be reversed, you cannot stop payment of the transaction.
  • You are responsible for keeping track of the transactions on the Savings Card.
  • You will not be paid interest on the funds in the card.
  • SuperCard* may change these terms and conditions without giving you notice.
  • In the event of a death. SuperCard requires a letter of authority from the master of the high court. This letter must be supplied by the Executor of the deceased’s estate.
  • If a customer loses a card, SuperCard or its agents will not be liable for any reimbursement of any nature.
