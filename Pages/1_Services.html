Our Services

SuperCard provides its customers with the opportunity to store their cash safely. There are NO transactional fees and only a R1.50pm admin fee is charged.

  • A user can register multiple card profiles

  • Deposit cash at the till

  • R1.50 will only be deducted if there are funds available

  • You can replace a lost card

  • Change your unique pin number

  • Withdraw a mini statement

  • Deposit a max of R5000 per week!
